Set a PIN to keep your spot

PINs are now required so you can recover your account if you change phones or clear your browser. Pick a 4-digit code you'll remember.

What is this? Why now?

Until today, PINs were optional and most accounts didn't have one. That meant a single browser refresh could lock you out forever. We're making them required for everyone. It takes 5 seconds.

Behaviour to validate: the modal is impossible to dismiss except by saving or signing out. Tapping the backdrop does nothing. There's no close button. The "What is this?" expands to explain. The "Sign out instead" escape exists for the rare user who'd rather start over than set a PIN.
